About BuzzRiding

Built for marketers in the middle of the shift.

Not beginners. Not C-suite. The working professionals navigating AI day-to-day — and doing it without a technical background to fall back on.

Why BuzzRiding exists

AI didn't arrive with a manual. It arrived fast, loud, and confusing — and most of what was written about it was either too technical, too hyped, or aimed at developers and executives.

Working marketers got left in the middle. Smart enough to know they needed to adapt. Busy enough to not have hours to waste on tools that don't deliver. Experienced enough to smell the nonsense in most AI content.

BuzzRiding exists for that person. Honest reviews of tools that are actually relevant to marketing work. Workflows you can follow without a technical background. Real experiments with real results — including the failures.

What we stand for

🎯

Practical first

Every article leaves you with something you can actually do. No theory for the sake of it.

🔍

Honest always

If a tool underperforms, we say so. Affiliate relationships never change what we write.

📊

Data over vibes

Claims backed by numbers, test results, or direct comparisons. Not vague assertions.

🚫

Zero jargon

AI concepts explained in plain English. If we use a term, we define it immediately.

Career & Skills

Experiments

News & Trends

Curated AI marketing news with a practitioner's lens. What matters, what doesn't, and why it affects your work.

Who reads BuzzRiding

Marketing executives, managers, specialists, and freelancers with 3–10 years of experience. Growth-oriented. Intellectually curious. Early adopters who prefer evidence over enthusiasm.

They don't need AI explained from scratch — they know the basics. What they need is someone cutting through the noise and showing them what's actually worth their time.

If that sounds like you, you're in the right place.
